7-Day Peru Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Lima

🌅 Morning

Arrive in Lima at 6:30 AM, and take a morning flight to Cusco (1-hour flight).

🌞 Afternoon

After arriving in Cusco, take a 1.5-hour car/bus ride to Ollantaytambo.

🌙 Evening

Explore the Ollantaytambo ruins, and enjoy the peaceful atmosphere of this small town.
