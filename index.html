7-Day Peru Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Lima

Explore the capital city and enjoy some Peruvian cuisine.

Day 2: Sacred Valley

Visit Pisac, Ollantaytambo, and the breathtaking scenery of the Andes.

Day 3: Machu Picchu

Take a train to Machu Picchu and explore the ancient ruins.

Day 4: Rainbow Mountain

Hike the stunning, colorful Rainbow Mountain.

Day 5: Cusco City Tour

Discover the historical sites and markets of Cusco.

Day 6: Huacachina Sand Dunes

Experience sandboarding and a sunset dune buggy tour.

Day 7: Back to Lima & Departure

Enjoy your last moments in Peru before heading home.
