7-Day Peru Itinerary

Day 1: July 3 – Arrive in Lima, Fly to Cusco, Travel to Ollantaytambo

  • Morning: Arrive in Lima at 6:30 AM, and take a morning flight to Cusco (1-hour flight).
  • Afternoon: After arriving in Cusco, take a 1.5-hour car/bus ride to Ollantaytambo.
  • Evening: Explore the Ollantaytambo ruins and enjoy the peaceful atmosphere of this small town.

Day 2: July 4 – Day Trip to Machu Picchu

  • Morning: Take an early train from Ollantaytambo to Aguas Calientes (1.5 hours), then catch the bus to Machu Picchu.
  • Afternoon: Spend the day exploring Machu Picchu, with options for hiking Huayna Picchu or Machu Picchu Mountain.
  • Evening: Return to Ollantaytambo by train in the evening.

Day 3: July 5 – Sacred Valley to Cusco

  • Morning: Travel from Ollantaytambo to Cusco (1.5 hours by car or bus).
  • Afternoon: Visit Plaza de Armas, Qorikancha, and Sacsayhuamán ruins in Cusco.
  • Evening: Dinner and relaxation in the San Blas district.

Day 4: July 6 – 7 Lakes of Ausangate Hike

  • Morning: Depart early for a 7 Lakes of Ausangate hike (about 3-4 hours by car).
  • Afternoon: Hike and explore the stunning lakes and Ausangate Mountain.
  • Evening: Return to Cusco for a well-deserved rest.

Day 5: July 7 – Rainbow Mountain

  • Morning: Early departure for the Rainbow Mountain trek (3-hour drive).
  • Afternoon: Trek to the top of Vinicunca to see Rainbow Mountain and enjoy the stunning scenery.
  • Evening: Return to Cusco in the late afternoon.

Day 6: July 8 – Cusco to Lima

  • Morning: Take a morning flight from Cusco to Lima (1-hour flight).
  • Afternoon: Explore Miraflores, Plaza Mayor, and San Francisco Monastery.
  • Evening: Dinner in Barranco, enjoying ocean views.

Day 7: July 9 – Huacachina Day Trip

  • Morning: Take a morning bus from Lima to Huacachina (4-5 hours).
  • Afternoon: Enjoy a dune buggy ride and sandboarding in Huacachina, explore Ica, or visit a local vineyard.
  • Evening: Return to Lima in the evening (4-5 hours by bus).

Summary of Transit Time

  • Lima to Cusco (1-hour flight)
  • Cusco to Ollantaytambo (1.5 hours by car/bus)
  • Ollantaytambo to Aguas Calientes (1.5 hours by train)
  • Ollantaytambo to Cusco (1.5 hours by car/bus)
  • Cusco to Ausangate (3-4 hours by car)
  • Cusco to Rainbow Mountain (3 hours by car)
  • Cusco to Lima (1-hour flight)
  • Lima to Huacachina (4-5 hours by bus)
