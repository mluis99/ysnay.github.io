7-Day Peru Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Lima

🌅 Morning: Arrive at 6:30 AM and take a morning flight to Cusco.

🌞 Afternoon: Drive to Ollantaytambo and explore the town.

🌙 Evening: Relax and visit the Ollantaytambo ruins.

Day 2: Machu Picchu

🌅 Morning: Take an early train to Aguas Calientes.

🌞 Afternoon: Explore Machu Picchu and hike Huayna Picchu.

🌙 Evening: Return to Ollantaytambo by train.

Peru Packing List

  • 🎒 Travel Backpack - Lightweight and durable
  • 🧥 Rain Jacket - Weather can change quickly
  • 🥾 Hiking Shoes - Comfortable for long walks
  • 💧 Hydration Powder - Stay hydrated in high altitudes
